About Me

The story behind
the specialist

I am an Email Marketing Automation Specialist based in Lagos, Nigeria, available immediately for worldwide remote opportunities. My focus is building automated email systems that work quietly in the background every day — recovering abandoned revenue, nurturing new subscribers, rewarding loyal customers, and winning back lapsed ones — without anyone having to manually press send.

I built my skills the deliberate way: by going directly into Klaviyo's live environment and building a complete automation system from scratch. Not a template walkthrough or a course exercise — real strategic decisions on flow architecture, conditional logic, engagement-based segmentation, HTML/CSS email development, and UTM attribution connected to Google Analytics 4.

Before transitioning into email marketing, I spent years in high-accountability professional environments — including as a Senior Healthcare Assistant — where documentation accuracy, process discipline, and reliable execution under pressure were non-negotiable daily requirements. These qualities translate directly into email marketing, where a misconfigured flow or a broken conditional split does not just look bad — it costs real revenue.

I am backed by seven industry certifications across email marketing, content strategy, inbound marketing, social media, and analytics — and a portfolio that proves I can execute, not just theorise.
