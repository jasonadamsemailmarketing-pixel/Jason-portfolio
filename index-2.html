About Me

The story behind
the specialist

I am an Email Marketing Automation Specialist based in Lagos, Nigeria, available for worldwide remote opportunities. My focus is building the kind of automated email systems that work quietly in the background — recovering revenue, nurturing subscribers, and driving repeat purchases without anyone having to press send.

I chose to develop my skills the deliberate way: by going directly into Klaviyo's live environment and building a complete automation system from scratch — not through a template walkthrough, but through real strategic decisions on flow architecture, conditional logic, segmentation, and email design.

Before email marketing, I spent years in high-accountability environments — including as a Senior Healthcare Assistant — where documentation accuracy, process discipline, and reliable execution were daily requirements. These qualities translate directly into email marketing, where a misconfigured flow or a broken conditional split doesn't just look bad — it costs revenue.

I am backed by seven industry certifications and a portfolio that proves I can execute, not just theorise.
